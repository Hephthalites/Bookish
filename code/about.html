Who are we?




Bookish is a non-profit organization that encourages people to donate their old books or books they no longer use in order to bring educational delight to many others. The goal of the bookish contributions program is to generate revenue for our aim of helping the less fortunate. Although books are a vital part of our lives, many of us still have trouble deciding what to do with them. When we decide it's time to let them go, we want to know they're going to a good home where they'll be able to continue to enhance and improve the lives of others. That is what bookish aspires to be.

Many people in our society do not have access to books or cannot appropriately deliver educational books to their children. Not only can you share your favorite books with others who actually need them through bookish, but others can also do so, and the educational and compassion chain will continue to grow, benefitting more and more people. You can donate your books by visiting us at the location shown in the contacts section or phoning us to have them picked up for you. Every book that is donated is much valued.

We strive to fulfill our human and religious commitments by delivering the most essential feature a person can have, education, to those who are in need, especially children. Every penny or contribution we get is appreciated, and we make every effort to deliver it to its designated beneficiaries as soon as possible. Also, we need individuals like you to continue to educate our civilization so that we can provide for it. This Sadaqah Jariyah is not to be missed.
